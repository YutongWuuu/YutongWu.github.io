Big Mahu Holiday Edition

This is a cartoon sticker pack prepared for the Year of the Tiger Spring Festival holiday. Big Mahu demonstrates through his actions that this is how a holiday should be enjoyed.

Big Mahu, means Big horse-like tiger or the clumzy one, references the character BoJack Horseman, showcasing a relaxed and casual style.

Designer: Senyuan Liu, Yutong Wu
