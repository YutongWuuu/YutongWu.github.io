Test Your Subconscious Image

My classmate and I collaborated to design an HTML5 advertisement for the XPPEN drawing tablet, using the popular 'Test' game to attract people to learn more about the product.

Painting can reflect people's subconscious. We based our approach on the 16 personality types model, match each result with a corresponding art style and characteristics. This design not only attracts our target audienceâ€”drawing tablet users, but also aligns with the brand's theme 'Dare to Dream, Express Your True Self,' encouraging everyone to use the XPPEN drawing tablet to depict their true, unique selves.

This project won a Third Prize in Tianjin Division of the 14th National College Student Advertising Art Competition in 2022.
