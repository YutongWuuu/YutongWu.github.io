Tea of Healing

This is an advertisement designed for Yunnan Baiyao's new product, a series of toothpastes with tea flavors. By Cinema 4D modeling, I designed 4 characters, each representing a different flavor.

This project won an Excellence Award in Tianjin Division of the 13th National College Student Advertising Art Competition in 2021.
